WEDDINGS

Every second someone, some where in this world is tying the knot and we believe that each marriage is unique in its own way. With a comprehensive team of photographers who specialize in Candid Wedding Photography, we provide complete photography solutions with product range from matrimonial portfolios, pre wedding shoots, destination weddings, post wedding shoots. Take a look at our gallery below which will show you a small sketch of our work.

Candid photography is essentially a style of photography where the moment is captured as it happens, without any pre-planned posing. Our experienced photographers equipped with specialized shooting equipments will be continuously on hunt looking for those special moments and emotions which will leave a lasting impression.

CHILDREN'S PORTRAIT

Photographing children and families are a real blessing. As a photographer, one can achieve so much with the warmth, love and comfort of a family. Our team, tries to achieve just that. We would come to the comfort of your home and click away for half-a-day and then gift you some photographs which you will cherish all your life. The gallery below shows a small sketch of our work with new-born and children.

You can book us for the day your new-born comes home for the first time, or when your child achieves a milestone (or any other special occasion). We also make family stories (Pre to Post pregnancy or Yearly growing-up story). Get in touch with us to know more about it and many more unique concepts.

TRAVEL AND TOURISM

Just like all other photographers of the world, we love to travel and explore and of course, we love to take pictures while we travel. And while we are at it, we love to create stories on our travels too. So the next time you want some landscape images for a coffee-table, or some amazing Panorama Images to put up on the walls of your office, you can come to us.

We also offer Photography Workshop services for your treks and travel tours. We specialize in Timelapse Photography which is a product specially designed for showcasing landscapes in the most unique way.
